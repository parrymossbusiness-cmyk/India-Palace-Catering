Dietary Intelligence

Sophisticated Protocols for Upscale Indian Catering Across Every Dietary Need

In Silicon Valley's diverse corporate landscape, dietary inclusivity isn't optional — it's essential. Our kitchen maintains rigorous separation protocols and certified preparation standards.

Plant-Forward Vegan

A complete plant-based menu that celebrates Indian cuisine's natural affinity for vegan cooking — no compromises, no afterthoughts.

  • Dedicated vegan prep station & cookware
  • Cashew-cream based curries & kormas
  • House-made paneer alternatives
  • Coconut milk dessert offerings
  • Full ingredient disclosure on labels
✦ Vegan Society Compliant

Halal Available Upon Request

All meat and poultry sourced exclusively from certified halal suppliers with full traceability from farm to service.

  • ISWA-certified halal meat suppliers
  • Separate storage & preparation zones
  • Full supply chain documentation
  • No cross-contamination protocols
  • Certification available upon request
✦ ISWA Halal Certified

Gluten-Free Assured

Comprehensive gluten-free options crafted with naturally GF Indian staples — rice, lentils, chickpea flour — with rigorous cross-contamination safeguards.

  • Dedicated GF preparation surfaces
  • Chickpea & rice flour naan alternatives
  • All sauces verified GF-safe
  • Clear buffet labeling system
  • Staff trained in allergen awareness
